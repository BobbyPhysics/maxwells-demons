    Current Project

    Simulating Liquid Argon: A study in Python molecular dynamics

    Abstract Your project is to write, in teams of three students, a Python molecular dynamics (MD) code to simulate liquid Argon. We want to study how varying some of the simulation parameters affects the accuracy of the simulations and extract thermodynamic properties from the simulations. You will write a short “letter”-style paper to communicate, discuss and summarize your reasoning and your results.
